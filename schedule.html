Vinyasa Yoga This popular class is based on the belief that mental and physical health are interrelated, and sould be conditioned together. All of the classes at Stript Soul Yoga attempt to coordinate breathing and physical movement, improve focus and concentration, and help participants regain control of their body and mind.
