Who We Are

We are a group of women who believe in living a life full of joy, gratitude, and free of judgment. We got together to create this beautiful space and share the gift of yoga with the community. These feelings inform and guide our core teachings and practices at Stript Soul Yoga, as we focus on helping students better connect with themselves through their yoga practice no matter what level you are at. We offer all levels vinyasa style classes, workshops, and private lessons to help all of our students achieve healthier bodies and minds.



“The success of Yoga does not lie in the ability to perform postures but in how it positively changes the way we live our life and our relationships.” T.K.V. Desikachar
